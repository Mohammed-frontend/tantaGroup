What is a home security system and how does it work

A home security system is a network of devices designed to detect unauthorized entry into a home or property. It typically includes sensors, alarms, cameras, and a central hub that connects to a monitoring service or your phone.

Can I access my surveillance camera footage remotely?

Yes, most modern surveillance cameras offer remote access through smartphone apps or web interfaces, allowing you to view live and recorded footage from anywhere with an internet connection
